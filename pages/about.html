About Me

Hi, I’m Mark Palmer, an independent web developer/software engineer, interested in making your next project a reality!

I live just outside of Nashville with my wife and dog. I currently work as a residential appraiser in the Middle Tennessee area. During the period of 2023 with the rapidly increasing mortgage rates I, like many others in the industry, found myself with less work and more time. I invested my time into learning to code.

As I’ve grown and learned more about myself in life, I’ve tried to use that knowledge to my advantage. I didn’t just randomly decide to try programming. I decided to check it out because I found it appealing, and I thought I could be good at it. I’m an artist, I have a talent for pattern recognition and observation, I like solving problems, and I’m relentlessly curious. All of these qualities line up as great strengths for a developer. I also believe in the idea of being a life-long learner. You should never stop challenging your mind. I admire that aspect of a career in the tech industry, you're constantly learning new things.

I enjoy programming. There is something very comforting about knowing that the machine will always be true. Much like how mathematics is a universal language, we can rely on the consistency of computers.

When I’m not coding, you can probably find me hanging with my dog Wilson, journaling, juggling, making art, or I might just be outta town… Take a look at my interests page to see more!
