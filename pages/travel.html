My Travel Adventures

My Experiences Abroad

My first time traveling abroad was in high school. I was in the Chinese foreign exchange program, and I, along with 4 other students, spent 2 weeks in China. It was an incredible experience. During my time in China, I was able to use my language skills, and appreciate the culture in a way that I had never even known was possible. Afterwards I knew that I had to see more... There is a whole other world out there, where everything is different, it gives you a perspective on life, that I think everyone should experience.
China

Wo ai Zhongguo! (I love China!) I spent most of my time in China in Beijing. Each student had their own host family that they stayed with. The last 2 days of our trip we visited Shanghai, before heading back home. Pictured here is me in Tianamen Square, outside the Forbidden City (2006).

Thailand

My wife and I spent a month exploring Thailand. From the big city (Bangkok) to the Jungles of the North (Chiang Mai), then down to the beaches of the Southern Islands. Thailand will always be a favorite of mine. Pictured here is a Thai demon figure at the Grand Palace in Bangkok. (2017)

New Zealand

New Zealand was gorgeous. The waters here were bluer than anywhere else I've seen in the world, and not just the beaches, rivers too. My wife and I spent 2 weeks traveling the Northern Island. Pictured here is us on top of the Tongariro Alpine Crossing. (2018)

Indonesia

My wife and I went to Bali to shoot a wedding, and of course made a vacation out of it. We visited Bali, then went north to the Jungles of Ubud. We also visited the Island of Ghili Air, where bicycles are the only allowed form of transportation. Pictured here is us by Kelingking Beach. (2019)

Saint Lucia

My wife and I are SCUBA fans. We visited Saint Lucia in part, to get our deep water diving certifcations. Sandals Resorts do not charge for SCUBA! Pictured here is us on a roadside covered area overlooking the town of Canaries on the Western Coast of Saint Lucia. (2022)

Italy

My wife and I had often talked of visiting Italy. It was our first European country to visit together, and my first time in Europe. We went from Milan, to Bellagio, to Rome, to Florence, and then Ischia. Every location was wonderful in its own way. Pictured here is us in the Piazza Michelangelo in Florence. (2023)
